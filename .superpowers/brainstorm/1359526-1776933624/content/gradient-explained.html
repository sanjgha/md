How to Read the Gradient Bar

The bar visualizes where the current price sits within today's range.

Visual Breakdown
AAPL — Bullish day, near high
● AAPL
low current high
178.2 → 186.59 → 188.5
Green portion = price moved up from open. Yellow = current zone. Red = remaining to high.
CVNA — Bearish day, near low
○ CVNA
low current ↑ high
348 → 358.55 → 365.2
Mostly red = sold off from open. Current price closer to low than high.
GSAT — Flat day
● GSAT
low current ≈ middle high
79.2 → 79.85 → 80.5
Near middle of range = consolidation/flat day.

Quick Reference:

Price moved up from open
Current price zone
Room to high

At a glance: more green = strong up day. More red = selling off.
