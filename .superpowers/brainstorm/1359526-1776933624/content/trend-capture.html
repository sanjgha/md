How the mini candle works:

  • Body color: Green if close > open, Red if close < open
  • Body size: Larger body = stronger trend, smaller = consolidation
  • Wicks: Show intraday excursions beyond open/close

This is literally a candlestick, just miniaturized. You can read trend at a glance without clicking the chart.

The mini candle captures trend — that's what traders are trained to read.
