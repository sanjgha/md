What Each Visual Tells You:

Sparkline (Time)

  • Upward slope = buying pressure
  • V-shape = dip was bought
  • Λ-shape = rally rejected
  • Jagged = volatility/choppiness

Range Bar (Price)

  • Dot on right = near highs (strength)
  • Dot on left = near lows (weakness)
  • Dot in middle = indecision
  • Bar width = today's volatility
