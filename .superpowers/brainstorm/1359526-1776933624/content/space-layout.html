Space Management Strategy:

  • Remove $ change column — Redundant when you have % change
  • Compress gaps — Reduce spacing between columns from 6px → 4px
  • Smaller fonts for low/high — Use 10px instead of 12px
  • Sparkline width — 56px (not 64px) saves 8px with good clarity

Option A (with low/high)

  • See intraday range at a glance
  • Support/resistance levels visible
  • ~270px total — fits in 260px pane with padding

Option B (no low/high)

  • Cleanest, most breathable

But: loses range info you'd see on chart anyway

My recommendation: Option A. Low/high are key intraday levels traders use.
